What is Image Consultancy?

People judge you in matter of seconds based on your first impression, it’s a possibility that they decide everything about you and form opinions based on it. Your first impression is your lasting impression. Image affects how people perceive you either boosting your confidence or diminishing it. Having a good image does not automatically happen. It needs to be polished, refined , takes a lot of time, energy and effort. From our clothes to our hairstyle, the way we carry ourselves, our image, our personality speaks for itself and it’s a brand in itself.

The quarter stone of image consulting are ABC.

- Appearance

- Behaviour

- Communication

Appearance, Behaviour and Communication. When these 3 elements work in sync, clients are transformed from inside out without changing who they are as a person.

Image is more than our outward appearance. It’s a whole combination of package that we epitomise as a human being. It’s the way we stand ; speak, and sound even the small unique things like our posture; gesture; facial expression, our manners; etiquettes; presentation skills! And for us it’s also your personal brand, i.e. what makes you stand out from the crowd and differentiates you from the rest.

It’s a series of calculations that take into account natural colour harmonies, figure analysis, body image, how to look slimmer, how to look taller, right colour, right pattern, right cuts, how to hide the problem area like arms, hips, stomach etc.

It’s also towards motivating to achieve your goal, etiquette training, building more confidence, positivity towards life.

My tagline is “Feel good to make a difference”, meaning not only I work with people on their outer appearance but I also work on their inner mind- set, helping them to attain high self-esteem and high confidence in themselves.



Who can be my client?

Clients are, anyone who are seeking that next level in themselves. Those who are trying to lose weight, young millennials who are going to college or are in early days of their career , ladies who want to look more presentable , business professionals. Anyone who is ready to feel good about themselves and invest in their well- being by lasting a good impression wherever they go and create a USP for themselves
